Our Core Values

Creativity

Creative is the heartbeat of Clim Studio

It shapes every stage of our work, from brainstorming to execution. We transform bold ideas into compelling visuals that reflect the essence of each project. This Commitment to imaginative thinking not only enhances our final outcomes but also enriches the entire creative process.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of Connection.

Collaboration fosters a vibrant environment where diverse talents come together to share ideas and build on each other's strengths. This synergy ignites creativity and drives us to produce exceptional work that resonates with audiences and clients alike.

Craftsmanship

For us, every step is an opportunity to make something extraordinary 🚀

From the creative work to the production process, every detail is handled with precision and care ensuring a seamless and artfully executed experience from start to finish. Our commitment to quality extends to how we treat our clients, crafting strong relationships built on trust and a shared pursuit of excellence.

Our Story in images

Our Story in
Words

Catalan-born Climent Canal, better Known as Clim, has been forging his own autonomous path in the animation industry since 2010.

His first personal project, make it better, went viral in 2011 - amassing over 2 million views worldwide. Soon after, he co-founded Sebas&Clim, working hands-on with illustration, motion, and mixed-media projects. He rapidly grew into a formidable Mixed-Media Director, before deciding to fly solo... And, thus!

Clim Studio was born. He's since raised into a full-blown production company that wins international awards and works with the best brands from across the globe.
